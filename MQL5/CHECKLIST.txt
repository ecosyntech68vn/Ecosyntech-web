## PRE-MARKET (5-10 phút)

### 1. Market Analysis
[ ] Check D1 timeframe - Identify trend direction
[ ] Check H1 timeframe - Confirm entry timing
[ ] Check for key levels (S/R, Supply/Demand zones)
[ ] Identify market phase (Trending/Ranging)

### 2. Session Analysis
[ ] Current session: ASIA / LONDON / NY / OVERLAP
[ ] Session volatility: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH
[ ] Major news: Check calendar

### 3. Confluence Check
[ ] EMA alignment (8 > 21 = Bullish, 8 < 21 = Bearish)
[ ] RSI zone (<30 oversold, >70 overbought)
[ ] ADX > 25 for trend strength
[ ] Support/Resistance level proximity

## ENTRY RULES

### Buy Setup (All must match)
[ ] Trend UP (EMA 8 > EMA 21 on D1 or H1)
[ ] Price at support zone or pullback
[ ] RSI < 50 (not overbought)
[ ] Confluence score >= 2/3

### Sell Setup (All must match)
[ ] Trend DOWN (EMA 8 < EMA 21 on D1 or H1)
[ ] Price at resistance zone or pullback
[ ] RSI > 50 (not oversold)
[ ] Confluence score >= 2/3

## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Position Sizing
[ ] Max Risk: 2% per trade
[ ] Lot = (Balance × 2%) / SL_Distance
[ ] Min Lot: 0.01 | Max Lot: According to balance

### Stop Loss
[ ] ATR-based SL: 2 × ATR
[ ] OR at nearest support/resistance
[ ] Min SL: 50 points

### Take Profit
[ ] TP = Entry + (SL × 2) minimum
[ ] Trail SL after +2R profit
[ ] Partial close at +3R (50% position)

## FILTER RULES

### Time Filter
[ ] Trade only: 07:00 - 22:00 server time
[ ] Avoid: 12:00-13:00 (Lunch)
[ ] Avoid: Friday afternoon

### Market Filter
[ ] Spread max: 25 points
[ ] Min ATR: 100 points
[ ] Not during high-impact news
[ ] Not in extreme volatility

## POST-TRADE

### After Entry
[ ] Log trade in journal
[ ] Monitor for 1-4 hours
[ ] Move to breakeven at +1.5R
[ ] Trail at +2R

### Exit Rules
[ ] TP hit: Full exit, log result
[ ] SL hit: Full exit, log result
[ ] Time-based: Exit if > 24 hours no TP/SL
