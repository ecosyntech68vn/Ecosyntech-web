## 4. BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS

### Contribution Margin by Tier
| Tier | Revenue | Variable | Contribution | CM % |
|------|---------|----------|--------------|------|
| PRO | 99K | 30K | 69K | 70% |
| MAX | 199K | 30K | 169K | 85% |
| PREMIUM | 299K | 30K | 269K | 90% |

### Weighted Mix Contribution
```
60% PRO + 25% MAX + 10% PREMIUM + 5% BASE
= (69K × 0.60) + (169K × 0.25) + (269K × 0.10)
= 41,400 + 42,250 + 26,900
= 110,550 VND/month average
```

### Break-Even Calculations
```
Monthly BEP = Fixed Costs ÷ Weighted CM
= 2,450,000 ÷ 110,550
= 22.2 ≈ 22 customers/month

Annual BEP = 22 × 12 = 264 customers
```

### BEP by Scenario
| Scenario | Monthly BEP | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| 100% PRO only | 36 customers | Conservative |
| 60% PRO/40% MAX | 22 customers | Base case |
| 100% PREMIUM | 10 customers | Best case |
